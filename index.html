Swedish

The praised and preferred massage of many patrons. Our therapists will reduce pain, stiffness, and stress with pressure of your choice--ranging from light to rigorous.

Deep Tissue

Similar to the Swedish massage, the Deep Tissue concentrates in releasing chronic tension, but with a more emphasized pressure. Specialists will penetrate to the deepest layers of muscle tissue to work out the kinks. Studies have shown Deep Tissue massages have been shown to lower blood pressure.

Thai

Embark on an ancient Eastern journey with the Thai massage, a full-body massage that focuses on energy lines throughout the body. This massage draws on yoga and accupuncture techniques to ensure blood flow stimulation and pain reduction.

Hot Stone

Polished lava rocks assist therapists in melting away tension in this massage. Heat penetrates deep into the muscles, promoting deep relaxation and circulation.
